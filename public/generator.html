Frequently Asked Questions

What formats can I export to?

You can export your final image as a PNG, JPEG, or PDF. PNGs support transparent backgrounds, while JPEGs allow you to control the quality to get a smaller file size.

How do I use this tool?

We have a detailed guide! Please check out the How to Use page for a full walkthrough of all the features.

Why doesn't the final image update when I check "Transparent Background"?

The live preview is for quick HTML/CSS feedback only. Settings like transparency, format, and quality are only applied during the final rendering. After changing a setting, you must click the "Generate Downloadable File" button again to see the result.
